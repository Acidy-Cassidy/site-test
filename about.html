About Us

Founded in 1989, Wynwood Garage carries a legacy of excellence in restoring classic British cars, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern technology.

Everything is done in-house, from our factory in Turkiye to the final details completed in Naples, FL. Our team ensures that every step of the restoration process meets the highest standards of quality and precision.

Our state-of-the-art factory in Turkiye where the initial restoration work begins.

Final touches and detailing done in our workshop in Naples, FL.

Disassembly and frame preparation.

Custom paint jobs and body work.

Modernization: Disk brakes, suspensions, power features.

Engine swaps and performance enhancements.
